Requirements and Downloads

When using Workforce in the office with the web app, or in the field with the mobile app, you must have an ArcGIS organizational account. Project owners need to be administrators or publishers in the organization in order to create Workforce projects. ArcGIS Online is supported. Portal for ArcGIS support is coming.

  • Firefox 49.0 and later
  • Internet Explorer 11.0 and later
  • Edge 37.0 and later
  • Safari 9.0 and later
