My Content

My Content gives you access to My Sites, My Layers, My Maps, My Reports and the Map Legend. When you click My Content, the dialog box appears on the left.

With the exception of the Map Legend, the features of My Content have the following options.

Go back to the My Content home page.

Click and enter a keyword to search.

My Sites

The sites you have created appear in the My Sites list. You can access your sites to view them on the map, create new folders, edit, share, move and delete sites. Click on the eye icon to view your site on the map. Click View Full Table to view the date, type and description.

To access My Sites, click My Content and click My Sites.

Create a new folder

You can create a folder for your sites.

  1. Click the create new folder button and the new folder appears at the top of the list.
  2. Type the name of the new folder.
    3. Now that the new folder is created and named, you can move sites into the folder.

Rename a folder or site

You can rename a folder or a site.

  1. Check the check box of the folder or site you want to rename and click the rename button.
  2. The name is highlighted. Type a new name.

Edit a site

You can edit the results of a site by choosing another location, editing a polygon, adding rings, drive times or bands and getting reports.

  1. Check the check box to the left of the site.
  2. Click the edit button.
  3. The dialog box appears for you to make the edits.

Share a site

You can share a site with your organization or group.

  1. Check the check box to the left of the site you want to share.
  2. Click the share button.

    The Share Site on ArcGIS Online dialog box appears.

  3. The title and description fields are populated based on your site. You can change these.
  4. Check the check box to include organizations or groups to share your site. By default, your map will be stored in ArcGIS Online.
  5. Click Share.

Move a site

You can move sites to different folders.

  1. Check the check box to the left of the site you want to move.
  2. Click the move button.

    The Move dialog box appears.

  3. Click the folder to move the site and click Move.

Delete a folder or site

When deleting a folder, you delete the folder and the sites in it.

  1. Check the check box to the left of the folder or site you want to delete.
  2. Click the check button.
  3. Click OK in the Confirm Delete dialog box.

Column view

You can select the viewable columns when the My Sites table is minimized.

  1. Click the arrow to access the order drop-down menu for date, description and type. Date is the default and changes depending on your selection.
  2. Select the column you want to view when the table is minimized. You can choose from Date, Description and Type.
  3. Select ascending or descending order within the column you've selected.

My Layers

My Layers allow you to access the business and imported points you've saved. Business points are from Business and Facilities Search and imported points are from Import File.

To access My Layers, click My Content and click My Layers. Select Business Points or Imported Points.

My Maps

My Maps provides access to your color-coded maps, smart maps and web maps.

Color-coded maps and smart maps are indicated by these icons:

To access My Maps, click My Content and click My Maps. Select Color-Coded Maps and Smart Maps or Web Maps.

View maps

You can access your map using view maps.

  1. Check the check box to the left of the map you want to view.
  2. Click view maps.

    In Web Maps, click the check box to the left of the map you want to view and it appears on the map.

Delete a map

You can delete maps that are stand alone or maps in folders.

  1. Check the check box to the left of the map you want to delete.
  2. Click the check button.
  3. Click OK in the Confirm Delete dialog box.

My Reports

My Reports allow you to access reports you've created in Standard Reports and PDF reports you've created using the PDF button on the tool palette. This provides a central location where you can open, move and delete them. You can also create new folders for reports.

To access My Reports, click My Content and click My Reports. Select from:

  • Ordered Reports—reports and all-in-one PDF reports that you've created in Standard Reports.
  • Custom PDF Map Reports—PDF reports you've created using Create pdf or image.

  1. Click the create new folder button and the new folder appears at the top of the list.

Column view

You can sort the date column when the My Reports table is minimized.

  1. Click the arrow to access the date order drop-down menu.
  2. You can sort the date by ascending or descending.

Map Legend

The Map Legend allows you to view all elements displayed on the map in one location. Use the arrows to access the different elements displayed on the map.

To access the Map Legend, click My Content and click Map Legend. The Map Legend can also be accessed from the tool palette.

To remove an item from the map, check the check box to the left of the map item.
